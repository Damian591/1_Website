Aesthetic Beauty Services

  • BREAST SURGERIES

    These procedures include breast augmentation (implants to enhance size), breast reduction (removing excess tissue for comfort), and breast lift (raising and reshaping sagging breasts), often performed for cosmetic or reconstructive purposes.

  • FACIAL SURGERIES

    Includes cosmetic procedures like facelifts, eyelid surgery, and brow lifts to enhance facial appearance.

  • NOSE SURGERY

    Also known as rhinoplasty, this procedure reshapes the nose for aesthetic or functional reasons.

  • BODY CONTOUR CORRECTIONS

    Involves procedures like liposuction or tummy tucks to reshape and enhance body contours.

  • CRYOLIPOLYSIS WITH CRYOSLIM HYBRID

    A non-invasive fat reduction technique using cold temperatures to freeze and eliminate fat cells.

  • INJECTIONS

    Typically refers to Botox or dermal fillers used to reduce wrinkles and add volume to the face.

  • BTL EXILIS 360

    A non-invasive body contouring and skin tightening procedure using radiofrequency and ultrasound technology.

  • BTL EMSCULPT

    A non-invasive procedure that uses electromagnetic energy to build muscle and reduce fat.
